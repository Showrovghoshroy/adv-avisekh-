About

About Advocate
Avisekh Das Gupta

Advocate Avisekh Das Gupta is a practicing lawyer before the Chattogram Judge Court with a strong commitment to protecting clients' rights and delivering practical legal solutions across criminal, family, and labour matters, as well as proceedings under the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Every legal matter is approached with careful preparation, attention to detail, and dedication to achieving the best possible outcome within the framework of the law. Clients receive honest counsel, clear communication, and representation grounded in strategy rather than guesswork.

His practice is built on the belief that effective advocacy begins with listening — understanding each client's circumstances before shaping a legal path forward.
